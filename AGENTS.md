# Project Guidance

## Design Context

### Users
SpermRace is for competitive players who want fast, skill-based rounds with real consequence. They arrive either as curious first-time players who need a zero-friction practice path, or as crypto-native players who want a sharp, credible entry into paid rooms. They are usually on desktop or mobile, they decide quickly, and they do not want to read a sales pitch before playing.

### Brand Personality
Ruthless. Premium. Predatory.

The interface should feel tense, lethal, and controlled rather than playful or cute. It should create focus, consequence, and confidence. The product voice should be short, direct, and unsentimental.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark-first interface with a cold laboratory / predatory arena feel. Use deep ink backgrounds, warm bone text, and a restrained gold-amber accent as the primary visual signal. Favor Orbitron for impact display moments and Outfit for supporting copy. Motion should feel like drifting, stalking, and closing space, not floating SaaS polish.

Reference direction:
- premium combat UI
- luxury-dark editorial rhythm
- microscope / arena tension

Anti-reference direction:
- not a SaaS dashboard
- not a sportsbook or casino lobby
- not meme-coin slop
- not neon cyan / purple AI gradients

### Design Principles
1. Game first, wallet second. The player must understand the fight before being asked to spend.
2. Make every word earn its place. Copy should be blunt, short, and skimmable.
3. Stakes are room selection, not pricing UI. Paid entry should feel like choosing a hunt, not browsing plans.
4. Keep the surface severe and controlled. Avoid decorative clutter, nested cards, and generic glow-heavy crypto styling.
5. Mobile is not a reduced afterthought. The core decision flow must remain obvious and fast on a phone.
